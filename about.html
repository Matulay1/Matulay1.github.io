My Story

Photography started as a curiosity, moments caught on a cheap phone camera, faces and lights I didn't want to forget. Over time, it became a way to tell stories no one noticed. I draw inspiration from quiet emotions, street life, and honest expressions. Now, it's what I breathe.

Specialty

I specialize in raw, unposed moments. Whether it's candid portraits, urban stills, or behind-the-scenes shots, I'm drawn to profiles and expressions that feel stolen from time. I work best when the subject forgets the camera is there.

  • Commissioned for campaign visuals, personal portraits, and editorial-style shoots.
